How do you use apps?

How to navigate downloading, deleting, and using apps and how to keep your information safe when doing so

October 29, 2024 Paige Frank

How to Download Apps on an Android/Tablet:

  1. Open the Play Store: Look for the Play Store icon in your phone or tablet’s menu and tap on it
  2. Log in or Create a Google Account: To download apps, you’ll need a Google account. This is the account used to access other Google services, like Gmail. If you don’t have one, you’ll be prompted to create one. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully to set up your Google account
  3. Search for the App: In the search bar at the top of the screen, type the name of the app you want, or browse through the apps listed on the main page
  4. Install the App: Tap the app you want and click ‘Install’ (if the app is free). The app will download and be added to your menu and home screen. If your home screen is full, swipe across to view additional screens where the app may appear
    • Tip: If you’re on a trusted website with a link to download their app, you can click it to go directly to the Play Store
  5. Use Wi-Fi When Downloading: Make sure you’re connected to a Wi-Fi network before downloading apps. Downloading over cellular data may cause additional charges to your phone network

How to Download Apps on an iPhone or iPad:

  1. Open the App Store: Look for the App Store icon on your device and tap to open
  2. Log in or Create an Apple ID: You need an Apple ID to download apps. This is your account for Apple services
    • How to Set Up an Apple ID:
      • Open Settings > tap Sign in to your iPhone
      • Select Don’t have an Apple ID or forgot it? and follow the prompts to create your Apple ID
      • You’ll need an email address and to set a password
  3. Search for the App: In the search bar at the top of the App Store, type the app name or browse the app categories
  4. Download the App: Tap ‘Get’ (if the app is free) or the price if it’s paid
    • If you choose a paid app, add a payment method to your Apple ID as instructed
  5. App Location: Once downloaded, the app appears on your home screen or additional screens if your home screen is full. Swipe across to find it
